About Javea Port Golf Society

Golf Without the Club Fees

Javea Port Golf Society is a friendly, welcoming group of golfers who share a passion for great courses and good company. We rotate across the best courses on the northern Costa Blanca, from championship layouts to shorter 9-hole challenges.

Unlike traditional golf clubs, we have no joining fees and no share schemes. Members pay a €25 quarterly fee — which funds prizes and maintains a cash balance to partially cover booking fees in advance — plus the green fee, which we negotiate at discounted rates. An occasional €5 contribution applies on trophy competition events. Open to all abilities and nationalities.

🏆 Tournament & Trophy Calendar

Alongside weekly competitions across the Costa Blanca, four times a year we raise the stakes with our prestigious trophy events — occasions that bring out the best in our members and create memories that last long after the final putt drops.

🏆

The Francis McNeil Memorial Trophy

One of our most cherished competitions — a fitting tribute to a much-missed member. A true test of skill and nerve, this is a day when reputations are made and rivalries renewed. Will your name be next on the trophy?

✈️

The Away Days Trophies

Twice a year the society packs its bags and heads out on tour — taking on some of the finest courses the region has to offer. Two trophies are up for grabs across the two away day events, making them among the most eagerly anticipated fixtures in the calendar.

🥇

The Captain's Trophy

One of the most prestigious events in the society year. Played in honour of the current Captain, this is a day that carries real weight — every member wants their name associated with this one.

🏅 New for 2026

The Society Trophy

Brand new for 2026, the Society Trophy is set to become the most coveted prize in the society. Only one player can claim the honour of being the inaugural winner — who will it be?
