Features
Everything your society needs
From floating event ideas to publishing results. The full captain loop, handled.
📣

Interest Sounder

Float 2-4 course options before you book. Members tap "I'd play" and you promote the winner to a real event. No more chasing replies in WhatsApp.

📅

Event Management

Create events with course, format, cost, and tee times. Signup cutoffs auto-fill. Members sign up and pay in one tap. Capacity enforced.

🏌️

9 Competition Formats

Stableford, Medal, Texas Scramble, Better Ball, Matchplay, Greensomes, Foursomes, Reverse Waltz — plus custom formats for your society.

👥

Team Generator

Snake-draft by handicap produces balanced teams in seconds. Nudge players between teams manually. Save as draft, publish when ready.

🏆

Results & Leaderboard

Enter scores per player or per team depending on the format. Publish results and members are notified instantly with the winner announcement.

💶

Course Rate Cards

Store your negotiated green-fee rates by season and player count. Auto-fill event cost when you pick a course and date. No more digging through emails.

🤝

Sponsors

Add sponsors with logos, links, and active date ranges. Link them to events as primary sponsor, supporting, or prize donor. Members see them front and centre.

🔐

Roles & Permissions

Six roles: Member, Vice-Captain, Captain, Treasurer, Admin, Sponsor. Captains run events. Treasurers handle payments. Admins manage members. No overlap.

📱

Works on Any Device

Responsive web app — no app store download needed. Works on phones, tablets, and desktops. Members log in with a magic link, no passwords.

How It Works
Up and running in minutes
No installation, no training manual. Register your society and invite your members.

Register your society

Pick a name, choose a plan, and you're the admin. 30-day free trial, no card upfront.

Invite your members

Share your society join code via WhatsApp or email. Members sign up with their email — magic link login, no passwords.

Create your first event

Pick a course, set the format, and open signups. The Interest Sounder lets you gauge demand before you commit to a booking.

Draw teams & publish results

Generate balanced teams, assign tee times, and publish to members. After the round, enter scores and the leaderboard goes live.
